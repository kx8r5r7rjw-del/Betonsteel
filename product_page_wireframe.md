# Product Page - Detailed Textual Wireframe

## Global Styles
- **Inherited from the main stylesheet:** White background, dark grey text, steel blue accents.

---

## 1. Breadcrumbs

- **Layout:** A single line of text above the main content.
- **Content:** "Home > Producten > Bouwstaalmatten > Bouwstaalmat B500A"

---

## 2. Main Product Section

- **Layout:** A two-column layout.

### Left Column (Product Image Gallery)
- **Main Image:** A large, high-resolution image of the product.
- **Thumbnail Images:** A row of smaller images below the main image, allowing users to switch between different views.

### Right Column (Product Information & Actions)
- **Product Title:** "Bouwstaalmat B500A" (Large, bold text).
- **SKU:** "Artikelnummer: BSM-001" (Smaller, grey text).
- **Price:** "â‚¬35,50" (Large, bold, steel blue text).
- **Short Description:** A brief, one-paragraph summary of the product's key features.
- **Specifications (a table or list):**
  - **Diameter:** 8 mm
  - **Maaswijdte:** 150x150 mm
  - **Afmetingen:** 5000x2000 mm
- **Quantity Selector:** A numeric input field with "+" and "-" buttons.
- **CTA Buttons (side-by-side):**
  - "In winkelwagen" (Primary CTA, steel blue background).
  - "Offerte aanvragen" (Secondary CTA, white background, steel blue border and text).

---

## 3. Detailed Description Section

- **Layout:** A full-width section below the main product section, using tabs.
- **Tab 1 (Description):**
  - A more detailed description of the product, including its applications and benefits.
- **Tab 2 (Specifications):**
  - A comprehensive table of all technical specifications.
- **Tab 3 (Downloads):**
  - Links to download product datasheets or certificates (PDFs).

---

## 4. Related Products Section

- **Layout:** A carousel or grid of 4-5 related products.
- **Each Product Card:**
  - **Image:** A thumbnail image of the related product.
  - **Title:** The name of the related product.
  - **Price:** The price of the related product.
  - **Button:** "Bekijk product" (A simple button or link).
