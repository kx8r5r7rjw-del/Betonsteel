# Homepage - Detailed Textual Wireframe

## Global Styles
- **Background:** White (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Primary Text Color:** Dark Grey (`#333333`)
- **Accent Color:** Steel Blue (`#4682B4`)
- **Font:** A clean, sans-serif font like Roboto or Lato.

---

## 1. Header (Sticky)

- **Layout:** Full-width, with a top bar and main navigation.
- **Top Bar:**
  - **Left:** Language switcher (NL / EN).
  - **Right:** Phone number and a "Request a Quote" button (steel blue background, white text).
- **Main Navigation:**
  - **Left:** Company Logo.
  - **Center:** Navigation links (Product Catalogue, Calculator, About Us, News, Contact).
  - **Right:** Search icon and a shopping cart icon.

---

## 2. Hero Section

- **Layout:** Full-width, edge-to-edge image.
- **Background:** High-quality photograph of reinforcement steel mats.
- **Content (centered, in a semi-transparent dark overlay):**
  - **H1 Title:** "Het grootste assortiment betonstaal van Nederland" (Large, bold, white text).
  - **Subtitle:** "Korte levertijden | Op maat geleverd | Volgens KOMO/Kiwa en Eurocode" (Smaller, white text).
  - **Buttons (side-by-side):**
    - "Bekijk onze producten" (Primary CTA, steel blue background).
    - "Vraag een offerte aan" (Secondary CTA, white background, steel blue border and text).

---

## 3. Featured Product Categories

- **Layout:** A grid of 3-4 responsive cards.
- **Each Card:**
  - **Image:** A clean product photo representing the category (e.g., "Bouwstaalmatten").
  - **Title:** The category name (e.g., "Bouwstaalmatten").
  - **Description:** A short, one-sentence description.
  - **Button:** "View Products" (Steel blue, full-width within the card).

---

- **Left Column:**
  - **Title:** "Bereken handig hoeveel bouwstaalmatten nodig zijn" (White text).

---

## 5. Latest News/Blog Snippet

- **Layout:** A grid of 3 blog post previews.
- **Each Preview:**
  - **Image:** A relevant image for the blog post.
  - **Title:** The blog post title.
  - **Excerpt:** A short, two-line preview of the content.
  - **Link:** "Lees meer" (A simple text link with an arrow icon).

---

## 6. Customer Testimonials

- **Layout:** A slider/carousel showcasing 2-3 testimonials at a time.
- **Each Testimonial:**
  - **Quote:** The customer's feedback in a large, italicized font.
  - **Attribution:** The customer's name and company.
  - **Rating:** A 5-star rating visual.

---

## 7. Footer

- **Layout:** A multi-column layout with a dark grey background and white text.
- **Column 1 (Contact Info):**
  - Company name, address, phone number, email.
- **Column 2 (Navigation):**
  - Quick links to the main pages of the website.
- **Column 3 (Company):**
  - Links to About Us, certifications, etc.
- **Column 4 (Social Media):**
  - Icons linking to the company's social media profiles.
- **Bottom Bar:**
  - Copyright information and links to the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.
