# Sitemap: Betonstaal E-commerce Website

This sitemap outlines the pages, sub-pages, and key functionalities for the Betonstaal e-commerce website.

## 1. Homepage (`/`)
- Hero banner with rotating value propositions
- Featured product categories section
- Calculator widget snippet
- Latest news/blog items preview
- Customer testimonials section
- Footer with contact info, social links, and navigation

## 2. Product Catalogue (`/producten`)
- Main category listing (e.g., Bouwstaalmatten, Betonijzer, Supportliggers)
- Sub-category pages (e.g., `/producten/bouwstaalmatten`)
- **Functionalities:**
  - Filtering by size, diameter, mesh size
  - Sorting by price, popularity
  - "Custom size" option leading to a dedicated form or tool

## 3. Product Detail Page (`/producten/:category/:product-slug`)
- Product image gallery
- Detailed product description and specifications
- Price per unit/meter
- **Functionalities:**
  - Quantity selector
  - "Add to Cart" button
  - "Request a Quote" button (for custom or large orders)
  - Related products section

## 4. Calculator Page (`/calculator`)
- Interactive tool for calculating required materials
- **Functionalities:**
  - Input fields for floor area (mÂ²), diameter, mesh size
  - Calculation of the number of mats and support beams needed
  - Option to add the calculated materials directly to the cart

## 5. About Us Page (`/over-ons`)
- Company history and mission
- Information on certifications (KOMO/Kiwa, Eurocode)
- Team member profiles (optional)
- Contact information

## 6. News/Blog Page (`/nieuws`)
- Listing of all news articles and blog posts
- Individual article pages (`/nieuws/:article-slug`)
- **Functionalities:**
  - Categorization of articles (e.g., lead times, export, price indices)
  - Search functionality

## 7. Contact & Service Page (`/contact`)
- Contact form
- Phone number and email address
- Live chat widget
- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) section
- Company address and map

## 8. Shopping Cart (`/winkelwagen`)
- Overview of items in the cart
- **Functionalities:**
  - Update quantities
  - Remove items
  - Apply discount codes
  - Proceed to checkout

## 9. Checkout (`/checkout`)
- Multi-step checkout process:
  1. Shipping information
  2. Payment method selection
  3. Order review and confirmation
- **Functionalities:**
  - VAT handling for national and international customers
  - Integration with payment gateways

## 10. Customer Account (`/account`)
- User login and registration (`/account/login`, `/account/registreren`)
- **Sub-pages (once logged in):**
  - Dashboard (`/account/dashboard`)
  - Order history (`/account/bestellingen`)
  - Address book (`/account/adressen`)
  - Account details (`/account/details`)

## 11. Static Pages
- Terms and Conditions (`/algemene-voorwaarden`)
- Privacy Policy (`/privacybeleid`)
- Disclaimer (`/disclaimer`)
